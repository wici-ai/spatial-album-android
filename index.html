Choose where the 3D is computed

Use the cloud, or keep it local.

Spatial Album can run against the hosted backend, or against a local backend on your own NVIDIA GPU.

Cloud

Sign in with Google, nothing to set up.

Use the hosted backend when you want the shortest path from download to orbit. The app handles cloud sign-in with Google.

  • No GPU required
  • Google sign-in in the app
  • Ready after the APK installs
Your own GPU (local)

Fully local, private, fast.

Run the backend yourself with one command. The app discovers it on your network automatically.

  • Your NVIDIA GPU
  • Photos stay on your network
curl -fsSL https://raw.githubusercontent.com/wici-ai/spatial-album-backend/main/deploy/install.sh | bash

Checks the GPU, pulls weights, starts the backend, and lets the app find it automatically.

How it works

A real 3D scene from one frame.

1

Photo

Pick a flat image from your album and keep the original frame intact.

2

Depth

SHARP estimates geometry and camera intrinsics from that single view.

3

3D splat

The backend converts the image into a Gaussian splat that renders in real time.

4

Reframe

Orbit, pan, refine, and generate a new composition from the scene.
