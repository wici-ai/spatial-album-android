How it works

A real 3D scene from one frame.

1

Photo

Pick a flat image from your album and keep the original frame intact.

2

Depth

SHARP estimates geometry and camera intrinsics from that single view.

3

3D splat

The backend converts the image into a Gaussian splat that renders in real time.

4

Reframe

Orbit, pan, refine, and generate a new composition from the scene.
